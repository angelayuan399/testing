Key Findings

  • Southwest Interior: ~5.5°C increase by 2100
  • Great Plains: ~5.0°C increase by 2100
  • Pacific Northwest: ~4.0°C increase by 2100
  • Coastal Regions: ~3.5°C increase by 2100
  • US Average: ~4.5°C increase by 2100

Interior regions warm more than coastal areas due to ocean buffering effects.
